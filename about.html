About GTA Scrub

Photo-verified commercial cleaning across the Greater Toronto Area.

Our Story

GTA Scrub was founded with a simple mission: bring transparency to commercial cleaning. We believe every business deserves to know exactly what was cleaned, how well it was cleaned, and when it was done.

That's why we created CleanCheck — our photo-verified reporting system that documents every clean with time-stamped photos, a 90-point checklist, and a satisfaction score.

Why Choose Us

We're fully insured and bonded. All staff are background-checked and trained on our proprietary cleaning protocols. We use eco-friendly products and hospital-grade disinfectants where required.

With 5,000+ cleans completed and a 4.9-star average rating, we're proud to be one of the fastest-growing commercial cleaning companies in the GTA.
