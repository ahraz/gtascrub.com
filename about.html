Our Story

GTA Scrub was founded with a simple mission: bring accountability back to commercial cleaning. Too many cleaning companies promise quality but deliver inconsistency. We decided to change that.

Our CleanCheck™ system photographs every cleaned area and scores it against a 90-point checklist. Our clients don't have to wonder if the job was done right — they can see it.

Headquartered in Brampton and serving 14+ GTA cities, we're a local company with a big commitment to quality.
