Our story

Founded in Brampton with a mission to change commercial cleaning

GTA Scrub was built on a simple insight: commercial cleaning companies ask you to trust them, but they never give you proof. We decided to change that.


Headquartered at 20 Glenfield Cres in Brampton, Ontario, our team brings over 30 years of combined experience in the janitorial and commercial cleaning industry. We've cleaned offices, medical clinics, retail stores, warehouses, and construction sites across every corner of the GTA.


Our founding belief is that accountability drives quality. That's why every Scrub Squad clean is documented with GPS-timestamped photos, scored against a 34-point checklist, and reported directly to your inbox within 30 minutes. We call it the CleanCheck system — and it's standard on every job, at no extra cost.
