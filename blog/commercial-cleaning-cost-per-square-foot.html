Commercial Cleaning Cost Per Square Foot in Ontario - 2025 Guide

June 8, 2025 · By GTA Scrub Team

Average Commercial Cleaning Costs in Ontario (2025)

Commercial cleaning prices in Ontario range from $0.08 to $0.25 per square foot depending on space type, frequency, and scope. For a standard 2,500 sq ft office in Brampton or Mississauga, this translates to roughly $200 to $625 per visit. Medical offices fall at the higher end due to infection control requirements. GTA Scrub offers flat-rate pricing with no hidden fees.

Office Cleaning Cost Per Square Foot

Standard office cleaning in the GTA ranges from $0.08 to $0.12 per square foot per visit, covering bins, dusting, vacuuming, and washroom sanitation. A 5,000 sq ft office in Toronto's Financial District would typically pay $400-$600 per weekly visit. Medical Office Cleaning Cost Per Square Foot

Medical and dental office cleaning ranges from $0.15 to $0.25 per square foot due to additional training and equipment required. Ontario PHO guidelines mandate specific sanitization protocols. A 1,500 sq ft dental clinic in North York might expect to pay $225-$375 per visit. GTA Scrub's medical teams receive specialized healthcare cleaning training.

Warehouse and Industrial Cleaning Costs

Warehouse cleaning typically costs $0.06 to $0.10 per square foot. A 30,000 sq ft warehouse in Brampton would cost $1,800-$3,000 per month for weekly service. Industrial cleaning requires heavy-duty equipment like ride-on scrubbers and high-reach tools.

Post-Construction Cleaning Pricing

Post-construction cleaning is priced per project rather than per visit. Commercial projects in the GTA range from $0.20 to $0.50 per square foot. A 2,000 sq ft retail space in Mississauga might cost $400-$1,000 for a thorough post-construction clean requiring HEPA vacuums and window cleaning.
