GTA Scrub CleanCheck: How We Prove Every Clean With Photo-Verified Reports

May 25, 2025 · By GTA Scrub Team

What Is the CleanCheck System?

CleanCheck is GTA Scrub's proprietary photo-verified quality assurance system. After every visit, our team photographs each cleaned area and scores it against a 90-point checklist. The result is a detailed quality report showing exactly what was cleaned and how well.

How CleanCheck Works

Each cleaner follows a standardized 90-point checklist customized to your space. After cleaning, they photograph every area — desks, washrooms, kitchens, floors, windows. You receive the report within hours. The process is completely transparent with no guesswork.

Why Photo-Verified Cleaning Matters

Traditional cleaning operates on trust. CleanCheck eliminates uncertainty with photographic proof. For property managers with multiple GTA locations, it provides consistent quality standards. For medical practices, it provides documented compliance evidence.

The 90-Point Inspection Checklist

The checklist covers entrance areas, workstations, kitchen sanitation, washroom cleanliness, floor care, windows, high-touch disinfection, and waste bins. Each item is scored pass/fail for an overall CleanCheck score targeting 90/100 or higher.

CleanCheck vs. Traditional QA

Most cleaners use subjective walkthroughs. CleanCheck is objective and transparent. The photo reports create a historical quality record you can track over time — apples-to-apples comparisons across all your locations.
