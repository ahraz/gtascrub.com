How Often Should Your Office Be Cleaned? A Guide for GTA Business Owners

June 10, 2025 · By GTA Scrub Team

Daily Office Cleaning: Who Needs It?

High-traffic offices, customer-facing retail spaces, medical clinics, and shared workspaces benefit most from daily professional cleaning. In Toronto's downtown core, offices with over 50 employees typically require daily service to maintain hygiene and appearance. Daily cleaning includes emptying all bins, sanitizing kitchen and washroom surfaces, wiping down high-touch points like door handles and elevator buttons, vacuuming high-traffic areas, and a quick dusting of visible surfaces.

Weekly Office Cleaning: The Sweet Spot for Most Businesses

For the average GTA office with 10-30 employees, weekly professional cleaning strikes the ideal balance between cost and cleanliness. Weekly service includes everything in a daily clean plus deeper vacuuming of carpeted areas, mopping hard floors, detailed dusting of blinds and ledges, cleaning interior windows, and restocking washroom supplies. Offices in Brampton, Mississauga, and Vaughan often fall into this category.

Bi-Weekly and Monthly Deep Cleaning

Some smaller offices or those with limited foot traffic may opt for bi-weekly or monthly professional cleaning. While this works for very small teams, we generally advise that bi-weekly cleaning often misses the mark on washroom hygiene and kitchen sanitation. Monthly deep cleaning is best as a supplement to weekly or daily service, focusing on carpet shampooing, floor stripping and waxing, and deep cleaning of appliances.

Factors That Affect Cleaning Frequency in GTA Offices

Several variables determine ideal cleaning frequency. Employee count directly correlates with how quickly a space gets dirty. The nature of your work matters too: medical offices require daily sanitization per Ontario PHO guidelines. Your location plays a role: downtown Toronto offices contend with more outdoor pollutants than suburban offices in Oakville or Richmond Hill. Seasonal factors like winter salt, spring pollen, and construction dust can all increase cleaning needs.

Office Cleaning Frequency by Season

GTA businesses should consider seasonal adjustments. Winter brings salt and sand into lobbies, requiring daily mopping of entrance areas. Spring and fall allergy seasons call for more frequent dusting and HEPA vacuuming. The smartest approach is a flexible cleaning agreement allowing you to increase frequency during peak months — exactly what GTA Scrub offers our clients across the GTA.
