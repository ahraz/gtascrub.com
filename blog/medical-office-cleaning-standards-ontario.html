Medical Office Cleaning Standards in Ontario - Compliance Guide

May 28, 2025 · By GTA Scrub Team

PHO Guidelines for Medical Office Cleaning in Ontario

Public Health Ontario sets rigorous standards for healthcare cleaning, emphasizing high-touch surfaces like examination tables and door handles. GTA Scrub's medical teams are trained in PHO-compliant protocols using hospital-grade disinfectants meeting Health Canada requirements.

Infection Control and Biohazard Disposal

Infection control prevents pathogen spread between patients. This includes colour-coded microfiber systems, proper disinfectant contact times, and biohazard waste disposal. GTA Scrub provides certified biohazard disposal for medical practices across the GTA.

Medical Office Cleaning Schedules

Medical offices require more frequent cleaning than standard commercial spaces. Waiting rooms need daily cleaning, and exam rooms need cleaning between patients. GTA Scrub recommends daily professional cleaning with our CleanCheck system documenting every visit.

Dental Office Cleaning Considerations

Dental offices face unique challenges with aerosols and bloodborne pathogens. PHO guidelines require specific disinfection for operatories and proper waste management. GTA Scrub's dental cleaning service includes these specialized protocols.

Choosing a Medical Cleaning Partner

When selecting a medical cleaning company, verify healthcare experience and quality assurance systems. GTA Scrub's CleanCheck provides photo-verified reports for every visit, giving medical practice owners confidence in their compliance.
