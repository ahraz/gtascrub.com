Office Cleaning vs Janitorial Services: What's the Difference for GTA Businesses?

May 22, 2025 · By GTA Scrub Team

What Is Janitorial Service?

Janitorial service is maintenance cleaning performed daily or frequently to keep a facility clean. Tasks include emptying bins, cleaning washrooms, mopping floors, vacuuming carpets, dusting surfaces, and restocking supplies. Most GTA businesses use janitorial services daily or several times per week.

What Is Office Cleaning?

Office cleaning goes deeper, addressing tasks that don't need daily attention: deep carpet cleaning, floor stripping and waxing, interior window cleaning, detailing kitchen appliances, and deep dusting of vents. Typically scheduled weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly.

Key Differences

Janitorial is high-frequency, surface-level maintenance. Office cleaning is lower frequency but greater depth. Most GTA businesses benefit from combining both: daily janitorial plus periodic deep cleaning. GTA Scrub offers integrated programs combining both services.

Why GTA Businesses Need Both

Janitorial alone leaves accumulated dust and grime that shortens carpet life and reduces air quality. Office cleaning alone cannot maintain daily washroom sanitation. The most cost-effective approach includes both services tailored to your needs.
