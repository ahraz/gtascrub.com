Post-Construction Cleaning Checklist for GTA Property Managers

June 1, 2025 · By GTA Scrub Team

Phase 1: Heavy Debris Removal

Before detailed cleaning begins, post-construction cleaning starts with removing large debris: drywall scraps, lumber offcuts, packaging, and empty product containers. This clears the way for detailed phases. GTA Scrub's teams coordinate with general contractors for optimal scheduling.

Phase 2: Dust Control and Vacuuming

Construction generates fine dust on every surface. Phase 2 involves HEPA vacuuming of ceilings, walls, light fixtures, window frames, and ledges. GTA Scrub uses commercial HEPA systems capturing 99.97% of particles, ensuring your renovated space is truly clean.

Phase 3: Window and Glass Cleaning

New construction leaves windows covered in construction film, paint splatter, and sticker residue. Phase 3 involves cleaning all glass surfaces with squeegees and professional-grade cleaner. Clean windows are the most visible sign of a thorough post-construction clean.

Phase 4: Kitchen and Washroom Deep Clean

Phase 4 focuses on detailed cleaning of plumbing fixtures, countertops, cabinets, and hardware. This includes removing adhesive residue, polishing fixtures, deep cleaning toilets and sinks, and sanitizing all surfaces — critical for making the space ready for daily use.

Phase 5: Floor Care and Final Polish

The final phase addresses floors and finishes. Hard floors need thorough mopping, carpets require final HEPA vacuuming, and some projects need stripping and waxing. GTA Scrub's CleanCheck system documents final quality with photo-verified reports.
