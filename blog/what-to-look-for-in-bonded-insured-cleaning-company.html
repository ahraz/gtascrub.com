What to Look for in a Bonded and Insured Cleaning Company in Toronto

June 5, 2025 · By GTA Scrub Team

General Liability Insurance: Your First Check

Every professional commercial cleaning company in Ontario should carry general liability insurance, typically $2 million minimum. This protects your business if the cleaning company causes property damage or someone is injured on-site. GTA Scrub carries $2 million liability insurance and provides proof to all clients before starting any contract.

WSIB Coverage: Required in Ontario

WSIB coverage is mandatory for cleaning companies in Ontario. Without it, your business could be liable if a cleaner is injured on your premises. GTA Scrub maintains active WSIB coverage for all employees. Always verify WSIB coverage before signing any cleaning contract.

Bonding: Protection Against Theft

Bonding covers your business in the rare event of theft by a cleaning employee. Not all cleaning companies are bonded. For GTA businesses in financial services or medical offices, working with a bonded company provides essential peace of mind. GTA Scrub is both insured and bonded.

Employee Screening and Training Practices

Ask how the cleaning company screens employees. Reputable companies conduct criminal background checks and provide comprehensive training. GTA Scrub conducts background checks on every team member and provides ongoing training in cleaning techniques and infection control.

Questions to Ask Before Hiring

When evaluating commercial cleaners in the GTA, ask: 1) Can you provide proof of insurance and WSIB? 2) Are employees bonded and background-checked? 3) Do you assign dedicated teams? 4) What quality assurance system do you use?
