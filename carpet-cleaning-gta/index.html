Commercial Carpet Cleaning in the GTA

Professional carpet cleaning — hot water extraction, spot treatment, and deodorizing for businesses across the GTA

Commercial carpets trap dirt, allergens, and bacteria over time. Regular vacuuming only removes surface debris — deep cleaning is essential for carpet health, indoor air quality, and professional appearance. GTA Scrub provides commercial carpet cleaning across Brampton, Toronto, Mississauga, and the entire GTA.

We use hot water extraction (steam cleaning) combined with commercial-grade cleaning solutions to remove embedded dirt, stains, and odors. Our truck-mounted or portable units deliver professional results every time, documented with CleanCheck™ photo verification.

Carpet cleaning includes: pre-inspection and spot treatment, hot water extraction, deodorizing, and rapid drying. All work is insured, bonded, and WSIB-covered.

Carpet Cleaning Services

Hot Water Extraction

Deep cleaning using hot water and cleaning solution injected under pressure, then extracted with a powerful vacuum. Removes embedded dirt, allergens, and bacteria. The industry standard for commercial carpet cleaning.

Spot & Stain Treatment

Pre-treatment of high-traffic areas and visible stains before full carpet cleaning. Specialized solutions for coffee, ink, food, and traffic patterns.

Deodorizing & Sanitizing

Eliminates odors and sanitizes carpets using commercial-grade deodorizers and disinfectants. Ideal for offices, medical clinics, and customer-facing spaces.

Carpet Cleaning Frequency

Industry standards recommend professional carpet cleaning every 6-12 months for commercial offices. High-traffic areas like lobbies, corridors, and reception areas may need quarterly cleaning.

Regular deep cleaning can extend carpet life by 2-3 years, saving thousands in replacement costs. Plus, it improves indoor air quality by removing allergens and dust trapped deep in carpet fibers.
