Dental Practice Compliance - North York

Dental Clinic North York 2,500 sq ft

The Challenge

Previous service lacked medical-grade disinfection protocols.

Our Solution

Project Overview

A North York dental practice needed a cleaning partner who understood PHO infection control standards for healthcare facilities. GTA Scrub deployed a specialized medical cleaning team trained in colour-coded microfiber protocols, hospital-grade disinfection, and biohazard waste disposal procedures.

After 18 months of service, the practice maintained 100% PHO compliance with zero infection incidents. CleanCheck scores averaged 96/100, and the practice reported improved patient confidence in their office hygiene. The ongoing relationship includes monthly quality reviews and CleanCheck reports that serve as compliance documentation.

Client quote: "GTA Scrub exceeded our expectations. The CleanCheck reports give us confidence that every corner of our facility is truly clean."

This North York dental practice continues to partner with GTA Scrub for ongoing daily cleaning and monthly deep cleaning services, relying on CleanCheck reports as part of their compliance documentation for PHO inspections.
