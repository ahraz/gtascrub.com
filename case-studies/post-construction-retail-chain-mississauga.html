Project Overview

A retail chain in Mississauga faced a critical deadline: their contractor had fallen behind schedule, leaving only 3 days for post-construction cleaning before the grand opening. GTA Scrub mobilized a 12-person team to complete all 5 phases of post-construction cleaning: heavy debris removal, HEPA dust control, window and glass cleaning, kitchen and washroom deep cleaning, and final floor care.

The space was ready for occupancy on time with a CleanCheck score of 91/100. The client was so impressed with the speed and quality that they signed a recurring cleaning contract for ongoing maintenance.

Client quote: "GTA Scrub exceeded our expectations. The CleanCheck reports give us confidence that every corner of our facility is truly clean."

The success of this post-construction clean led to a recurring maintenance contract with the retail chain's facilities management team, who now use GTA Scrub for all new store openings across the GTA.
