Project Overview

A 30,000 sq ft warehouse in Brampton's Industrial Park needed industrial-grade cleaning. The previous provider could not handle the scale — airborne dust was affecting inventory quality and equipment reliability. GTA Scrub deployed ride-on scrubbers, HEPA backpack vacuums, and an industrial dust control program tailored to the facility's layout.

Within 30 days, airborne dust levels dropped by 90%. CleanCheck scores averaged 89/100 with zero missed cleanings in 6 months of service. The facility manager reported noticeable improvements in air quality, working conditions, and inventory cleanliness.

Client quote: "GTA Scrub exceeded our expectations. The CleanCheck reports give us confidence that every corner of our facility is truly clean."

The Brampton facility continues to receive weekly industrial cleaning service, with quarterly deep cleaning of rack systems and mezzanine areas. CleanCheck reports are shared with the facility manager after every visit.
