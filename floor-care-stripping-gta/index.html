Floor Care, Stripping & Waxing in the GTA

Professional floor restoration — stripping, waxing, buffing, and sealing for commercial spaces across the GTA

Your commercial floors take a beating daily. Foot traffic, furniture movement, and spills gradually wear down floor finishes, leaving them looking dull and unprotected. GTA Scrub's floor care services restore and protect your flooring investment across Brampton, Toronto, Mississauga, and the entire GTA.

We work with all common commercial flooring types — vinyl composition tile (VCT), hardwood, laminate, tile, concrete, and rubber. Our floor care technicians use professional-grade machines and finishes to deliver showroom-quality results.

Floor care services include: stripping and waxing, buffing and burnishing, deep scrubbing, sealing, and polishing. All work is documented with CleanCheck™ photo verification before sign-off.

Floor Care Services

Stripping & Waxing

Complete removal of old floor finish followed by application of new, high-grade wax. Ideal for VCT, linoleum, and vinyl floors. Restores shine and protects against future wear.

Buffing & Burnishing

Maintenance buffing and high-speed burnishing to restore gloss between full strip-and-wax cycles. Extends the life of your floor finish and keeps floors looking fresh.

Hardwood & Concrete Sealing

Professional sealing and refinishing for hardwood floors and concrete surfaces. Protects against moisture, stains, and daily wear in high-traffic commercial spaces.

Floor Care Schedule

Most commercial floors benefit from stripping and waxing every 12-18 months, with buffing and burnishing every 3-6 months to maintain appearance between full cycles.

High-traffic areas like lobbies, hallways, and retail floors may need more frequent service. Our free assessment will recommend the right schedule for your space.
