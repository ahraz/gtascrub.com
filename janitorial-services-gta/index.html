Janitorial Services in the GTA

Complete janitorial programs — nightly cleaning, restroom sanitation, and waste management across Brampton, Toronto, Mississauga & all GTA cities

A well-maintained facility starts with reliable janitorial services. Whether you need nightly cleaning for a multi-tenant office building or daily maintenance for a retail space, GTA Scrub delivers consistent, high-quality janitorial service across Brampton, Toronto, Mississauga, and the entire GTA.

Our janitorial programs are designed to maintain cleanliness between deep cleaning cycles. Every visit is documented with CleanCheck™ photo-verified reports, so you always know the state of your facility.

Janitorial services include: nightly cleaning, trash removal and bin liners, restroom sanitation and restocking, floor sweeping and mopping, surface dusting, common area cleaning, and supply management. All staff are insured, bonded, and WSIB-covered.

Janitorial Programs

Nightly Janitorial

Our most popular program — nightly cleaning after business hours so your space is fresh and ready each morning. Includes trash removal, restroom cleaning, floor care, and common area maintenance.

Multi-Tenant Building Cleaning

Janitorial services for office buildings with multiple tenants. Includes lobby and common area cleaning, elevator cleaning, window cleaning, and coordinated tenant suite cleaning schedules.

Retail & Customer-Facing Cleaning

High-visibility janitorial for retail stores, restaurants, and customer-facing businesses. Focus on entrance areas, sales floors, fitting rooms, and washrooms to maintain a welcoming environment.
