Medical & Dental Office Cleaning in the GTA

Infection control, biohazard disposal, and PHO-compliant cleaning for healthcare facilities across the GTA

Medical and dental offices require more than standard cleaning — they need infection control, biohazard management, and compliance with Public Health Ontario (PHO) guidelines. GTA Scrub provides specialized medical office cleaning across Brampton, Toronto, Mississauga, and the entire GTA.

Our teams are trained in healthcare cleaning protocols, including colour-coded microfiber systems to prevent cross-contamination, hospital-grade disinfectants, and proper handling of biomedical waste. Every clean is documented with our CleanCheck™ photo-verified system.

Medical office cleaning includes: examination room sanitization, waiting area disinfection, biohazard waste disposal, high-touch point cleaning, colour-coded microfiber cleaning, and compliance documentation. All cleaning staff are insured, bonded, and WSIB-covered.

PHO Compliance & Infection Control

Public Health Ontario sets strict standards for medical office cleaning. Our protocols are designed to meet or exceed these requirements, giving you confidence during inspections and audits.

We use a colour-coded microfiber system — green for exam rooms, blue for waiting areas, yellow for washrooms — to eliminate cross-contamination risks. All disinfectants are hospital-grade and meet Health Canada guidelines for healthcare settings.

Our CleanCheck photo-verified reports provide documented proof of cleaning compliance, which is invaluable for PHO inspections and liability protection.
