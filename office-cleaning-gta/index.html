Commercial Office Cleaning Services in the GTA

Professional office cleaning with photo-verified quality reports — serving Brampton, Toronto, Mississauga & 11 more GTA cities

First impressions matter. A clean office tells clients you care about professionalism and tells employees you care about their health. GTA Scrub provides comprehensive office cleaning services across Brampton, Toronto, Mississauga, Vaughan, Markham, and every other GTA city.

Every office clean comes with our CleanCheck™ photo-verified quality report. We photograph every cleaned area and score it against a 90-point checklist. You see the proof before you pay — no more wondering if the cleaning actually got done.

Office cleaning includes: desk and workstation wiping, vacuuming carpets, mopping hard floors, kitchen and break room sanitation, washroom cleaning and restocking, trash removal, and high-touch point disinfection. All our cleaning teams are insured, bonded, and WSIB-covered.

Office Cleaning Frequencies

Daily Office Cleaning

Best for high-traffic offices, customer-facing spaces, and businesses with 50+ employees. Includes daily trash removal, surface sanitizing, vacuuming high-traffic zones, and washroom cleaning. From $0.08/sq ft

Weekly Office Cleaning

The sweet spot for most GTA offices with 10-30 employees. Everything in a daily clean plus deeper vacuuming, mopping all floors, detailed dusting, interior window cleaning, and supply restocking. From $0.10/sq ft

Bi-Weekly & Monthly

Ideal for smaller offices or as a supplement to daily/weekly service. Focuses on deep cleaning tasks: carpet shampooing, floor care, behind-furniture cleaning, and appliance deep cleans.
