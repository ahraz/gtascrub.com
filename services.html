Our Cleaning Services

GTA Scrub provides 8 specialized commercial cleaning services across the Greater Toronto Area. Every service includes CleanCheck photo-verified reports, dedicated teams, and flat-rate pricing — no hidden fees.

Office Cleaning

Daily, weekly, or bi-weekly cleaning for offices of all sizes. Includes desks, floors, kitchens, washrooms, and trash removal. Dedicated teams and CleanCheck photo reports with every visit.

From $0.08/sq ft

Medical Office Cleaning

Infection control, biohazard disposal, and compliance-focused cleaning for dental clinics, medical offices, and healthcare facilities. Meets Ontario health standards.

OHSA & PHO compliant

Post-Construction Cleaning

Heavy debris removal, drywall dust control, window cleaning, and final polish. We work with your timeline to get spaces move-in ready fast.

Warehouse & Industrial Cleaning

Large-space cleaning for warehouses, distribution centres, and industrial facilities. Sweeping, scrubbing, dust control, and waste removal.

Window Cleaning

Interior and exterior window cleaning for commercial buildings. Streak-free results using professional-grade equipment.

Floor Care & Stripping

Hardwood, tile, vinyl, and concrete floor stripping, waxing, buffing, and sealing. Restore your floors to like-new condition.

Carpet Cleaning

Hot water extraction, spot treatment, and deodorizing for commercial carpets. Extends carpet life and improves indoor air quality.

Janitorial Services

Comprehensive janitorial programs including nightly cleaning, restroom sanitation, supply restocking, and waste management.
