Warehouse & Industrial Cleaning in the GTA

Large-space cleaning for warehouses, distribution centres, and industrial facilities across the GTA

Warehouses and industrial facilities face unique cleaning challenges — large square footage, high dust accumulation, heavy machinery, and strict safety regulations. GTA Scrub provides specialized warehouse cleaning across Brampton, Mississauga, Toronto, and the entire GTA.

Our industrial cleaning teams use high-capacity equipment designed for large spaces, including ride-on sweepers and scrubbers, HEPA-filtered vacuums, and industrial-grade cleaning solutions. Every clean is documented with CleanCheck™ photo verification.

Warehouse cleaning includes: floor sweeping and scrubbing, dock area cleaning, dust control on racking and shelving, trash and waste removal, washroom and break room sanitation, and mezzanine cleaning. All teams are insured, bonded, and WSIB-covered.

Warehouse Cleaning Costs

Warehouse cleaning is typically priced per square foot. In the GTA, warehouse cleaning costs range from $0.06 to $0.10 per square foot depending on frequency, space size, and scope of work.

A 30,000 sq ft warehouse in Brampton with weekly service would cost approximately $1,800-$3,000 per month. We offer free on-site estimates to provide accurate pricing for your specific facility.
