Commercial Window Cleaning in the GTA

Streak-free interior and exterior window cleaning for commercial buildings across Brampton, Toronto, Mississauga & all GTA cities

Clean windows transform the appearance of your commercial property. They let in more natural light, improve the view for employees, and create a professional impression for clients. GTA Scrub provides commercial window cleaning across Brampton, Toronto, Mississauga, and the entire GTA.

Our window cleaning teams use professional-grade equipment including purified water systems, squeegees, and extension poles to reach every window safely. We clean both interior and exterior surfaces, removing dirt, grime, construction film, and hard water spots.

Window cleaning includes: interior and exterior window washing, frame and sill cleaning, glass door cleaning, streak-free finishing, and ground-level debris cleanup. All teams are insured, bonded, and WSIB-covered.

Window Cleaning Frequency Guide

Monthly — Best for retail storefronts, restaurants, and ground-floor offices where appearance directly impacts customer perception.

Quarterly — Ideal for standard office buildings, medical clinics, and multi-tenant commercial properties.

Bi-annually — Suitable for upper-floor windows, warehouses, and industrial facilities where exterior appearance is less critical.
